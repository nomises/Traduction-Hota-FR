===== Map name =====
Strife in the Woods

===== Map description =====
The alliance between the lords was very unstable. The knights, barbarians, and swamp dwellers were all waiting for the right moment to break the treaty and benefit as much as possible from this loathsome deed. When wizards settled in the hills, the balance was disrupted, and the alliance crumbled. P
